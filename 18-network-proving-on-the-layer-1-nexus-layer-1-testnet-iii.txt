Testnet III Overview
Testnet III is now live
and represents the current phase of Nexus testing. This testnet focuses on security, decentralization, and orchestration testing.

How Testnet III Works
In Testnet III, users earn
NEX Testnet III Points
by proving via Nexus OS or CLI and then
claim
them to convert them into
NEX Testnet III Tokens
.

Proving via Nexus OS or CLI
When you start to prove via Nexus OS or CLI, you will earn NEX Testnet III Points.
Because Testnet III is a new environment, you will start with 0 Testnet III Points and earn them through proving.
